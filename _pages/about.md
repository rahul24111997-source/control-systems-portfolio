## Hello, I'm Rahul S

I am a **Control Systems Engineer** with a **Master of Engineering in Control Systems** from **PSG College of Technology** and a **Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical and Electronics Engineering**.

I am passionate about designing intelligent industrial automation systems, developing advanced control algorithms, and solving engineering challenges using modern software and industrial tools.

My technical interests include:

- MATLAB Programming
- Simulink Modeling & Simulation
- PLC Programming
- Control System Design
- ETAP (Basic & Advanced)
- AutoCAD (2D & 3D)
- Industrial Automation
- Technical Problem Solving

I enjoy designing, simulating, testing, and improving engineering systems while continuously learning new technologies used in modern industries.
